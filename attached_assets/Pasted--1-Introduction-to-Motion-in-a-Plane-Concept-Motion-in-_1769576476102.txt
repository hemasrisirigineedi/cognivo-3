🔹 1. Introduction to Motion in a Plane
Concept

Motion in a plane means motion in two dimensions (x and y).
To describe such motion, vectors are required because direction matters.

Kinematics: Study of motion without considering forces.

Example

A car moving east → straight-line motion
A ball thrown at an angle → motion in a plane ✔️

🔹 2. Scalars and Vectors
Definitions

Scalar: Quantity with only magnitude
👉 Distance, speed, time, mass

Vector: Quantity with magnitude and direction
👉 Displacement, velocity, acceleration, force

Example

Speed = 10 m/s ❌ (no direction)

Velocity = 10 m/s east ✔️
