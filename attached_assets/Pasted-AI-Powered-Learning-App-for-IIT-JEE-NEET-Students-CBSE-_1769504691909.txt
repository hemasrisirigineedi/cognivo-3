AI-Powered Learning App for IIT-JEE & NEET Students
(CBSE + NCERT Based, Chapter-wise Smart Learning Platform)
1. Product Overview

Build a full-stack AI-powered learning app for IIT-JEE (Main & Advanced) and NEET aspirants, based entirely on the official CBSE / NCERT syllabus.

The app should function like NotebookLM, but instead of users uploading PDFs, all NCERT content is preloaded, structured, and continuously updated.

The platform must provide:

Chapter-wise NCERT content

Key exam-focused highlights

Video explanations

Audio/podcast-style summaries

Quizzes & flashcards

AI chat support for doubt solving

2. Target Users

Class 11 & 12 students preparing for:

JEE Main

JEE Advanced

NEET

Students who want structured, exam-oriented learning, not random videos.
